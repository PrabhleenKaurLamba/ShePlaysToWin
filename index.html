She Plays to Win

By: Prabhleen Kaur Lamba

About the Book

She Plays to Win will be published as a softcover and E-book by New Degree Press in August 2021 and the hardcover edition will be available in November! All proceeds will be donated to the Women's Sports Foundation and Bay Area Women's Sports Initiative.

The book shares the inspiring journeys of female athlete leaders and explores the fight for equal pay, media coverage, and respect in women's sports. Prabhleen wrote this book because she has been playing sports since elementary school but realized that many of my friends, especially girls, did not have this opportunity. She also grew up watching women’s sports and noticed the lack of media coverage and unequal pay professional female athletes have to deal with. Her hope is that, in reading this book, you will discover the true potential of girls and women’s sports. She hopes to instill in you the same passion and excitement that she has for women’s sports and wants you to see this book as a tool to help you be a part of the fight for gender equity.

About the Author

Prabhleen Kaur Lamba is a high school student athlete and advocate for girls and women’s sports. She is passionate about giving back and making a lasting influence on her community. As a female athlete herself, and growing up watching sports, Prabhleen hopes to create more opportunities for young girls and women to play sports. She also wants professional female athletes to be given the recognition they deserve and work so hard for. In her free time, Prabhleen enjoys cheering on her favorite sports teams with her friends and family. She also loves playing sports, her favorites being basketball and crossfit.
